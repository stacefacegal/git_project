THE WOLF

August 26, 2013

The new Wolf is a rugged, eight-eyelet boot that blends combat style with modern fashion and the comfort of a sneaker sole. The three new styles just released are all constructed using premium materials and durable stitching. A heel zipper allows for easy on and off, and the raw interior and padded tongue give an authentic combat boot feel. The upper rests on a modern cup sole, with durable sidewall stitching, and a unique SUPRAFOAM midsole lies beneath the footbed providing all-day comfort and cushioning. Black Satin TUF on the vamp and eye stay is complemented by black Block-Stitch TUF. Black rope laces, black metal eyelets, and a translucent cup sole.
